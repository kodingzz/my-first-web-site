Universe

The giant ancient Bracken Skarner is revered in Ixtal as a founding member of the ruling class of Yun Tal. Dedicated to keeping his country safe from the outside world, Skarner lives in the inner chambers beneath Ishao-Khan, listening to the vibrations of the earth and detecting potential threats. As more and more members of Yun Tal begin to question Ishtal's isolation, Skarner's paranoia becomes increasingly severe. Skarner will do anything to keep Ixtal and its people safe. No matter what the cost.
