Champion

Sivir is a renowned mercenary leader in the desert region of Shurima. Wielding a jewel-encrusted cross-blade sword that appears in legends, he has won numerous battles and has never disappointed customers who paid expensive commissions. Sivir also takes great pride in finding treasures buried in the catacombs of the old Shurima Empire. Of course, I don't forget to receive a large compensation... As the ancient beings who led the Shurima Empire return, Sivir also finds herself at a crossroads.
