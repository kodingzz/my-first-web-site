Garen: The First Shield

The King has passed away. Demacia was overcome with grief. Garen Crownguard is blaming himself for the current situation.
But will Garen be able to do it when he has to step up for Demacia once again?
While scouting across Demacia's borders for peaceful purposes, Garen, Quinn, and the Indomitable Vanguard learn of a plot to break an old alliance. While Mobile Knight Quinn works to inform the capital, Garen and his fellow vanguard members make a desperate last stand. How long can Garen and the Indomitable Vanguard hold out? And at what cost?
